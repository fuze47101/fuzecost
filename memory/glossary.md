## Competitors (what FUZE is NOT)
| Competitor Type | Problems |
|----------------|----------|

| Copper compounds | EPA registered pesticide, aquatic toxicity |
| Zinc pyrithione | H400/H410 classified, ZDHC restricted |
| Quaternary ammonium (quats) | EPA pesticide, VOCs, binders, antimicrobial resistance |

## Key Differentiators
- Zero binders, surfactants, VOCs, solvents
- No heat curing needed
- 1m² production footprint, 30-amp laser, solar-capable
- Recycled electronics as feedstock
- Fabric hand feel unchanged
- Textiles remain recyclable (not embedded)
- Sub-ppm loading vs 100-10,000x more in competitors
